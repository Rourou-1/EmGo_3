📋 Tab 1 — Events
Your Command Center for All Events
The Events tab is the first thing you see after logging in. It's where you create, organize, and manage every event you're running. Think of it as your master list — everything else in EmGo flows from here.
＋
Create Events
Click + Create Event (top right) to open the New Event modal. Give your event a name, date, type (Professional, Social, Personal, or Community), color accent, and description. All four types are color-coded for quick scanning.
⊞
Grid & List Views
Toggle between Grid and List view using the sub-tabs below the filter bar. Grid shows visual cards with your event's color accent and key stats; List gives a compact row layout for quick scanning.
🔍
Search & Filter
Use the search bar to find events by name. Use the filter chips — All · Pro · Social · Personal · Community · Finished — to narrow the list. The Sort dropdown lets you order by Date, Name, Status, or Type.
✅
Finished Events
Events marked as Finished are automatically moved to a collapsible section at the bottom, keeping your active events clean and focused. Finished events become read-only across all tabs — venue, schedule, map, and attendees are all locked.
📊
Overview Sidebar
The left sidebar shows a live Overview of your selected event: total Attendees (green), Venue Layers, Activities on the schedule, and any scheduling Conflicts (red). These numbers update automatically as you work.
💡
Pro tip: Click any event card to make it the active event — all other tabs (Schedule, Venue, Map, Attendees) then show data for that specific event only.

📅 Tab 2 — Schedule
A Visual Weekly Timeline for Your Event
The Schedule tab shows a weekly calendar grid for the currently selected event. You can plan every activity, set exact times, assign colors, and instantly see if any two activities overlap.
📆
Week Navigation
Use the ← Prev and Next → arrows at the top to move between weeks. The column headers show the actual date for each day, keeping your planning anchored to real calendar dates.
＋
Adding Activities
Click any empty time slot on the grid to open the Add Activity modal. Set the title, start and end time, day, color, and any notes. The activity block immediately appears in the grid at the correct position and height based on its duration.
✏️
Editing & Deleting
Click any existing activity block to open its Edit modal. Change the title, time range, day, color, or notes. You can also delete the activity directly from the edit modal using the Delete button.
⚠️
Conflict Detection
If two or more activities on the same day overlap in time, EmGo highlights them in red and increments the Conflicts counter in the Overview sidebar. Resolve conflicts by adjusting start/end times so activities no longer overlap.
⏱
Adjustable Time Range
The grid spans from 6 AM to 12 AM by default. The time range adapts to your activities — early morning or late-night events are fully supported. Time labels on the left use a clean 12-hour AM/PM format.
💡
Pro tip: The Activities and Conflicts counts in the Overview sidebar are live. Keep an eye on them as you build out your schedule to spot issues early.

🗺 Tab 3 — Venue
Design Your Floor Plan on a Live Canvas
The Venue tab is a full interactive canvas editor where you design a visual layout of your event space. Place rooms, tables, stages, walls, and more — then organize them across multiple floor layers.
▭
Element Palette
Pick from 8 built-in elements in the right panel: Room, Table, Stage, Wall, Entry, WC, Exit, and Custom Shape. Click an element to select its tool, then click and drag on the canvas to draw it. Each element has a preset size that you can resize after placing.
↖
Select, Move & Resize
Switch to the Select tool (V) to drag elements around. Click an element to show resize and rotate handles. Drag any corner handle to resize, or the circular handle at the top to rotate. Hold Ctrl and click multiple elements to select them as a group.
⊞
Layers System
Use Layers (right panel, top) to organize multi-floor venues. Add a new layer with the + button. Each layer can be shown or hidden independently, so you can focus on one floor at a time without visual clutter.
🎨
Colors & Custom Elements
Select any color from the 8-color palette before placing an element, or use the Custom color picker for any hex value. Use ✦ Custom Shape to draw freeform polygons, rectangles, lines, or circles. Use 🖼 Custom Element to upload an image and place it on the canvas.
⬇
Zoom, Grid & Export
Zoom in/out with the − / + buttons or scroll wheel. Press ⊡ Fit to reset the view. Toggle the alignment grid with G for precise placement. When your layout is ready, click ⬇ Export to save it as an image file.
⌨️
Keyboard shortcuts: V Select · P Pan · R Room · T Table · S Stage · W Wall · G Grid · Ctrl+Z Undo · Ctrl+C/V Copy/Paste · Del Delete · Q Pan/Select

📍 Tab 4 — Map
Pin Real Locations on a Live Interactive Map
The Map tab uses an interactive Leaflet map to let you drop, name, and share geographic pins for your event locations. Each pin is tied to your active event and can be navigated to via QR code or share code.
📌
Adding & Managing Pins
Click anywhere on the map to drop a new pin, or use the Add Pin button in the side panel. Give each pin a name, address, color, and optional event association. Pins appear as color-coded markers on the map and are listed in the panel.
📷
QR Code Scanner(For Mobile)
Tap the Scan QR button to open your device camera. Point it at any EmGo-generated QR code, and the map will instantly fly to that location and open a popup with the pin's name, address, and share code. Only QR codes generated by EmGo are recognised.
#️⃣
Enter Share Code(For Mobile)
Each pin has a unique short alphanumeric Share Code. Tap Enter Code, type the code (e.g. AB12C), and the map flies directly to that pin. This is useful when sharing a location verbally or in a message, without needing the full QR.
🗺
Map Navigation
Pan by clicking and dragging. Zoom with scroll, pinch, or the +/− controls. Click any pin marker to open a popup showing its name, address, and share code. The map automatically centers on your active event's pins when you switch events.
🔗
Share & Export
Generate a QR code for any pin using the QR button in the pin list. The QR code encodes the pin's exact coordinates, name, address, and share code — anyone with EmGo can scan it to jump straight to that location.
💡
Pro tip: Pin codes are case-insensitive. You can share them in all-caps or lowercase — EmGo normalises them automatically when resolving a location.

👥 Tab 5 — Attendees
Manage Everyone Coming to Your Event
The Attendees tab is your guest list manager. Add, edit, and track every person attending the active event — with roles, statuses, and contact info all in one place.
➕
Adding Attendees
Click + Add Attendee to open the Add modal. Fill in the person's first name, last name, email address, role (e.g. Speaker, Organizer, Guest, Staff), and status (Confirmed or Pending). All fields are saved to the active event's attendee list.
✏️
Editing & Removing
Click any attendee row or card to open the Edit modal. Update their details or change their status as things evolve — for instance, moving someone from Pending to Confirmed after they respond. Use the Delete button to remove them from the list.
🔍
Search & Filter
Use the search bar to find attendees by name or email. Filter chips let you view only Confirmedor Pending attendees at a glance — handy for following up with people who haven't confirmed yet.
🏷
Roles & Statuses
Roles help you categorise attendees by function: Organizer, Speaker, Staff, Guest, etc. Statuses — Confirmed or Pending. The total confirmed attendee count is reflected in the Overview sidebar.
💡
Pro tip: Each tab — Schedule, Venue, Map, and Attendees — is always scoped to the active event selected in the Events tab. Switch events from the left sidebar or the Events tab cards.
